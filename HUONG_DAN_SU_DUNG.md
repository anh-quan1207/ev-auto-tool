# Huong Dan Su Dung EV Auto Tool

## 1. Cai dat lan dau

1. Mo thu muc phan mem.
2. Bam `install.bat`.
3. Neu may chua co Node.js, he thong se mo trang tai Node.js.
4. Cai Node.js xong thi bam lai `install.bat`.
5. Cho den khi thay thong bao cai dat hoan tat.

## 2. Mo phan mem

1. Bam `start.bat`.
2. He thong se tu mo backend, frontend va trinh duyet.
3. Neu trinh duyet khong tu mo, vao dia chi:

```text
http://localhost:5173
```
